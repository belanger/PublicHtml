Grading criteria:

The grading will be based on the following:

  • Whether or not the solutions are written the way people read; left to right, top to bottom.
  • Whether or not you've identified everything you've written down.
  • Whether or not you've used notation correctly.
  • Whether or not you've justified what you've written down.
  • Whether or not you've written the answer at the end, as the conclusion, clearly identified.
